# LEVEL 0 CHARACTERS

Level-0 characters represent ordinary people before becoming adventurers.

They are fragile and poorly equipped.

If they survive early adventures they may advance into full ICRPG classes.

## Ability Modifiers

Roll 3d6 for each stat.

Modifier results

3 → −2
4–6 → −1
7–13 → 0
14–17 → +1
18 → +2

Stats

STR  
DEX
CON
INT
WIS
CHA

## Level-0 Hit Points

Roll starting HP.

HP = 1d4 + 2 + CON modifier

Minimum HP = 1.

When a Level-0 character earns their first Milestone they become Level-1 and gain 1 full Heart (10 HP).

Previous HP does not carry forward.

## Background

Roll on **Level-0 Background Table**.

Each background provides

• a weapon appropriate to the profession
• one profession item

Example

Merchant — Dagger, Coin purse (d6×10 Coin)

## Effort

Level-0 characters use **Basic Effort**.

## Advancement

When a Level-0 character earns their first Milestone they become Level-1.

Choose a class and follow normal ICRPG advancement rules.

# HERO COINS

Each PC begins every session with **1 Hero Coin**.

PCs cannot gain additional personal Hero Coins.

The party begins with **1 shared Hero Coin**.

The party may gain **one additional Hero Coin** during play.

Maximum party pool = 2.

Party coins may be gained from

Natural 20 on any roll
Major discovery
Major victory
Defeating a boss enemy
Major story development

# EQUIPMENT AND LOAD

Items are divided into **Equipped Items** and **Packed Items**.

Load capacity is determined by Strength.

## Carry Capacity

Load capacity

10 + STR modifier

## Equipped Items

Equipped items are immediately usable.

Examples

weapons
shields
belt gear
worn armor
held light sources

Equipped item limit

5 + STR modifier

## Packed Items

Packed items are stored in containers.

Accessing packed items requires time during exploration or combat.

## Containers

No Container

Maximum carried items = Equipped item limit

Backpack

Maximum carried items = Load capacity

Sack

Maximum carried items = Load capacity +4 slots

## Item Load

Light item → 1 slot
Standard item → 2 slots
Heavy item → 3 slots
Very heavy item → 4+ slots

# CAROUSING

A character may carouse in a settlement.

Minimum cost = 10 Coin.

Gain +1 to the roll for every additional 10 Coin spent.

Roll on **Carousing Table**.

# NPC GENERATION

Use this procedure whenever the PCs interact with an unknown person.

Draw a **GMA card**.

Determine

Name → use card name
Encounter context → Situation line
NPC role → interpret belongings
Personality → trait line

Determine attitude

Roll on **NPC Attitude Table**.

Determine power

Roll on **NPC Power Table**.
